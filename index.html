Record

The power of a database with the simplicity of a spreadsheet.

Record is the simplest way to organize everything.
It’s like a spreadsheet but built for your phone.

Tired of messy notes and complicated spreadsheets? Record lets you create a custom home for your data in seconds. Whether you're tracking a collection, managing your small business inventory, or keeping a simple log, you can build the perfect structure without any coding.

KEY FEATURES

  • Fully Customizable Fields: Choose exactly what to track. Add text, numbers, dates, or checkboxes for every record.
  • Two Ways to View:
    • Visual List: See your data with big, beautiful pictures—perfect for catalogs and collections.
    • Pro Table View : View your data in a clean grid for fast, seamless visual comparison. Perfect for reviewing large data sets on the go without dealing with tiny, frustrating Excel cells.
  • Universal Search: Find anything instantly. Our search scans every field in your list, so no detail is ever lost.
  • No Technical Skills Needed: If you can use a list, you can use Record. It’s powerful like a database but as easy as a notebook.

WHAT CAN YOU TRACK?

  • Inventory & Stock: Keep your business items organized.
  • Personal Collections: Books, games, sneakers, or plants.
  • Task Lists: Advanced checklists with dates and notes.
  • Daily Logs: Expenses, workouts, or habits.
